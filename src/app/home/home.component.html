IMPACT

This application is designed to let you send activities to your students at times when they could help. A fun and refreshing way to lower student stress and promote well-being.

Research shows that 10 weeks of the affirmation process is the optimal time to create real, lasting change.

The activities are designed for students 10 years old and up, including college students.

To participate, students need to be able to read and write.

Because the activities will be sent through email or text, they also need to have either an email account or a mobile phone.
