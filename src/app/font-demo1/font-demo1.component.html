HOW IT WORKS

This application is very simple. Giving students a chance to write about important values in their lives has been shown to help them stay resilient and perform up to their potential. But there’s a catch: they need to do these activities at the RIGHT time—before a stressful event. That’s where this app comes in.

Here are the three steps that will deliver the activities to your students for maximal benefit.

Record a date when you think students might feel stressed.

This could be the date of a test or homework assignment, or maybe it’s just the beginning of the school year, or RIGHT NOW.

Create a roster to identify which students you need to help.

You don’t have to get too specific here. There’s no harm to sending these activities to everyone. So the roster you create could be all the students in your classroom or a few students that you work with.

Send activities to the roster of students.

That’s all you need to do. We’ll take it from here. We’ll send the activities to students to complete and we will keep you updated how it went. We’ll also send your students a brief survey before and after the activity to assess its impact, and we’ll share the results with you. If you like, you can see the activities before we send them.
